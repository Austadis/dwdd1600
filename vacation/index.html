Who Are We?

We are a Family-based adventure company that strives to find the best adventure for you and your family! Our main goal is to help families to reconnect and to enjoy the outdoors while spending time together.

We started as a small family wanting to reconnect after having a hard time coming to grips with the reality of social media and the way that it tends to disconnect us from our loved ones. We decided to use social media to the benefit of families, and create this vacation matcher to help people find personalized adventures for their individual families.

Some of our most popular destinations include the Caribbeans, Switzerland and Italy
