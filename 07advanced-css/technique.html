Climbing Techniques

Bouldering

  1. Legwork needs to be on-par
  2. Arms should be straight
  3. Breathing needs to be level
  4. When falling - fall backwards

Lead Climbing

  1. Trust your belayer
  2. Breathing needs to be level
  3. Use a figure 8 follow through knot

Terms for Noobs:

Bouldering
A form of free climbing that is performed on small rock formations or artificial rock walls without the use of ropes or harnesses
Lead Climbing
A technique in rock climbing where the lead climber clips their rope to the climbing protection as they ascend the climbing route, while their second remains at the base of the route belaying the rope to protect the lead climber in the event that they fall
Belay
A variety of techniques climbers use to create friction within a climbing system, particularly on a climbing rope, so that a falling climber does not fall very far
