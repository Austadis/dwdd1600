Climbing Techniques

Bouldering

  1. Legwork needs to be on-par
  2. Arms should be straight
  3. Breathing needs to be level
  4. When falling - fall backwards

Lead Climbing

  1. Trust your belayer
  2. Breathing needs to be level
  3. Use a figure 8 follow through knot
